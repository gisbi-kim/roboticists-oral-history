# Jodi Forlizzi — Career Timeline

> Source: ETHW Oral History Interview (`ethw_source_eng/forlizzi.md`)

```
Birth                — Born in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, USA; grew up in Harrisburg
Undergrad            — BFA in illustration, Philadelphia College of Art (University of the Arts)
1985~1995            — Information Designer at the UPenn School of Engineering (started as an undergrad), collaborating with theoretical physicists and materials engineers
1986~1996            — Founder of Inks Creative Services in Philadelphia
Master's years       — Master's in Interaction Design at CMU; won an Apple student competition with a GPS hiking-stick project; visits to Interval/Apple
1997                 — MS in Interaction Design from CMU
~1997                — Design Researcher at the CMU Novum Design Center
1998~1999            — Innovator/Project Manager at E-Lab LLC in Chicago (user-research consulting)
Early 2000           — Joined CMU as assistant professor in the HCI Institute / School of Design (the HCI Institute's first designer)
Early 2000s          — Joined the Nursebot project; 4 months of elderly user research → redesigned the head (collaborating with Carl DiSalvo and Francine Gemperle)
Early 2000s          — Won a large ITR grant; first collaboration with Reid Simmons
Subsequent research  — Hug (a remote-communication plush toy), SenseChair (a posture-aware chair), Roomba home study
Mid-period collab    — Bilge Mutlu collaboration on the Aethon TUG hospital robot (contrasting cancer wards vs. postpartum wards)
2007                 — Self-defined Design + HCI PhD from CMU
Around 2006          — Started independent development of Snackbot (a 4-year design-led robot)
HRI formation period — Co-launched the ACM HRI Conference and ran the young researchers workshop with Sara Kiesler and Pam Hinds
Around 2010          — Design collaboration on the HERB robot (Sidd Srinivasa); user research with young people with spinal cord injuries
2011-01              — Snackbot pilot scheduled to start (at time of interview)
At time of interview — In year 6 of QoLT (Quality of Life Technology Center); on multidisciplinary grants with Manuela Veloso, Howie Choset, Reid Simmons, etc.
2010-11-23           — IEEE oral history interview in Pittsburgh, PA
2014                 — Promoted to Full Professor at CMU
Consulting           — Advisor to Willow Garage, Disney Research, etc.
```
